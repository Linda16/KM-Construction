Clarke Contractor CON1450WC 150mm Wall Chaser - 230V

 

Cuts clean channels into plaster, brick, thermalite block, concrete etc. for laying electric cables. Saves time and effort whilst providing accuracy. Ideal for electricians and plumbers. Can also be used for DIY.

  • Adjustable cutting depth 8-40mm and width 8-30mm to suit various applications.
  • 150mm Twin diamond blade.
  • Break out chisel for clearing debris leaving clean channels.
  • Soft Start. Spindle lock.
  • Dust extraction facility.
  • Supplied with handy carry case, side handle, chisel and two diamond blades.
  • 1450Watt
